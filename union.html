作品介绍

Introduction

在手机日益普及的当下，许多人沉迷于使用手机而忽视了和身边人的交流，我们希望通过我们的设计作品去推动人与人之间的互动；让人们在信息化时代回归到生活的交流本身。人应该借助于产品达到更好的生活，而不是被束缚在冰冷机器的虚拟乐趣中。 

With the increasing popularity of mobile phones, many people are addicted to using mobile phones and ignore the communication with people around them. We hope to promote the interaction between people through our design works.

     Let people return to the exchange of life itself in the information age. People should use their products to achieve a better life, instead of being bound in the endless virtual fun of cold machines.
